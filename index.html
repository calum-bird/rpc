How It Works

RPC works by using your device to connect to a remote server in the closest large city to you which allows your device to become much more powerful than it really is. This allows you to play PC games on your android device, or use CAD software on your iPad!

The concept is actually quite simple, and makes sense. This platform allows computers to be more accessible and cost-effective for everyone! So why hasn't it been done already? We don't know, and we're here to change that.
